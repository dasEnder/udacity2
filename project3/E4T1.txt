

# Identify 2-3 changes that can be made to our environment to prevent an ssh brute force attack from the internet.

1. Remove ssh user/password access or ssh access at all. Use a bastion, VPN, or Systems Manager; alternative move to inmutabel instances.
2. After (2), remove all direct internet access to the applcation instance.
3. Enable WAF and apply policies that automatically block suspicious traffic.
